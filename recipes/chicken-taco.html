Chicken Taco Seasoning

Description

This chicken taco seasoning lets you discard the pre-made packets,
so that you choose exactly what goes into the mix.
This makes enough to season approximately 6 pounds of cubed chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup chili powder
  • 2 tablespoons onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried cilantro flakes
  • 1 teaspoonKosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon frshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Combine chili powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, cumin, oregano, cilantro, salt, and pepper in a mixing bowl. Stir to combine. Transfer to an airtight container; store for up to 3 months. Use in any taco recipe.
