The BEST Homemade Lemon Pepper Seasoning

Description

This is the BEST homemade lemon pepper seasoning.
African yellow pepper and white pepper both bring heat to the usual mix of lemon zest and black pepper,
for a lemon pepper seasoning that's perfect on chicken, fish, or roasted vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 6 lemons, zested
  • 1 tablespoon white pepper
  • 1 tablespoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon ground African yellow pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder

Steps

  1. Place lemon zest in a shallow dish or on a cookie sheet. Add white pepper, black pepper, African yellow pepper, salt, and garlic powder; mix well.
  2. Using a fork or wooden muddle, press down on lemon zest to release the lemon oil and essence. Refrigerate any lemon pepper that is not used immediately in a spice jar with an airtight lid for up to 1 month.
