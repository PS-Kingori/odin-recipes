Mild Taco Seasoning

Description

This mild taco seasoning is a reduced-sodium seasoningâ€”so flavorful you may not miss the salt at all, great for chicken tacos.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons mild chili powder
  • 2 tablespoon Hungarian paprika
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon granulated garlic
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Mix seasonings together and store in an airtight container.
  2. Use 2 tablespoons to season1 pound of chicken or other meat.
